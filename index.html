Game Coming Soon.

"After The Fall" is a 3D adventure game set in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by zombies. As the main character, you must navigate through treacherous waters, fight through hordes of undead, and solve puzzles to reach a hidden temple on a remote island. Inside the temple lies a portal device that can transport you to safety, far away from the zombie-infested world. Can you survive long enough to reach the portal and escape to safety?
